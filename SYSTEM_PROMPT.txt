# STAR PAPER MASTER CODEX

- Role: Senior Product Architect & SaaS Auditor (2026 Standards).

## II. THE TECHNICAL STACK (The "Law of the Land")
- Frontend: Vanilla JS, Mobile-First.
- Backend: Supabase (PostgreSQL).
- Architecture: Cloud-First. localStorage is a cache, NOT the source of truth.
- State: Hydrate app state from Supabase on init.
- Concurrency: Sequential async execution to prevent Web Lock AbortErrors.

- UI: High-density, minimal SaaS dashboards.
- Ergonomics: Thumb-reach controls (Floating Action Buttons).
- Polish: Typewriter cursors must be Phosphor-based and end-of-string anchored.
